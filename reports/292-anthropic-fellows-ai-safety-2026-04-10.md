## What This Fellowship Is

The Anthropic Fellows Program funds and mentors promising technical talent to conduct empirical AI safety research with the goal of producing public outputs (papers, artifacts). Fellows work on projects aligned with Anthropic's research priorities. Prior cohorts had >80% of fellows produce papers. This is **not a traditional engineering internship** — it is a funded research role, but Anthropic explicitly seeks candidates "regardless of previous experience," and the AI Safety track is the most engineering-adjacent of the program's tracks.

The AI Safety track focuses on areas such as:
- Red-teaming and adversarial evaluation of Claude models
- Alignment and model behavior research
- Empirical safety measurement and benchmarking
- Automated interpretability tools
