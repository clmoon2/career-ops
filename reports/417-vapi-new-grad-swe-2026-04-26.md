## Company

Vapi is the leading voice AI infrastructure platform — APIs and SDKs for building production voice agents (real-time telephony, WebRTC, TTS/STT orchestration). Used by thousands of developers to deploy voice AI at scale. Seed to Series A stage ($20M+ raised), ~50 engineers, based in San Francisco, CA. Backed by Y Combinator. High-velocity culture: "Founder Juice" — the team ships fast and expects the same from early hires.

**Context:** Vapi processes millions of voice AI calls via its infrastructure. The company is a category-defining platform for voice agents (analogous to what Twilio was for SMS/voice a decade ago, but AI-native).

## Role

**Full-time new grad position, San Francisco, CA (in-person).**

Responsibilities:
- Build production-ready features across TypeScript/React/Node.js stack
- Work directly alongside world-class engineers on Vapi's core platform
- Understand customer use cases and translate them into product decisions
- Participate in brainstorming sessions and ship autonomously

Requirements:
- BS/MS in Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, or related field
- Proficiency in TypeScript, React, and Node.js
- Strong communication (technical and non-technical stakeholders)
- Startup readiness: "a reason to be there and making sacrifices for something uncertain"

The role emphasizes early ownership: new grads ship to production fast, with minimal hand-holding. This is not a rotation program.
